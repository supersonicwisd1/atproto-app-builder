Platforms take your data, your identity, your choices.   The AT Protocol lets you take them back — build decentralized web apps for yourself, your friends, or the world.

Their Web

CENTRALIZED

The application owns your identity and data

  • Each app owns all data about you — and won't let you see or change it
  • You can't leave because everyone you know is locked in
  • If you leave anyway, your data stays behind
  • Algorithms optimize for “engagement” — even through manipulation and hate

Your Web

DECENTRALIZED

You own your identity and data

  • You own your data — change it however you like
  • Leave any app without losing your connections
  • Take your data with you wherever you go
  • Choose apps that offer the experience you want
