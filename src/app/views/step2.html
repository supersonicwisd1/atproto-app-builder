Define Your App's Requirements

In the App Wizard, requirements come in three forms:

  1. InformationThink about this question from the perspective of someone using your app: what do I need to know?
  2. Data interactionsThese will be in the form, "I need to [verb] [data]". For example, "I need to see a list of items", or "I need to search from my list of items."
  3. NavigationOnce you've added some different views in your app, you can create navigation flows between them.
